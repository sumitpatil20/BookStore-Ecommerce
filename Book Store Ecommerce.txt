Database (MySQL):
create database bookStore;
Table :
	users (user_id , name , email , password , role , created_at)
	books(book_id , title , author , price , description , image_url , stock , created_at)
	cart(cart_id , user_id , created_at)                                   // user_id references users(user_id)
	cart_items(cart_item_id , cart_id , book_id , quantity)                // cart_id references cart(cart_id)        // book_id references books(book_id)
	orders(order_id , user_id , total_amount , status , created_at )       // user_id references users(user_id)
	order_items(order_item_id , order_id , book_id , quantity , price )    // order_id references orders(order_id)    // book_id references books(book_id)
	payments(payment_id , order-id , payment_method , payment_status , transaction_id , payment_date)                 // order_id references orders(order_id)
	contact_messages(message_id , email , subject , message , created_at);

Execution Flow 
1)  SignIn flow 
SignIn.jsx
    ↓
authService.js
    ↓
Backend /auth/signin
    ↓
JWT token + role received
    ↓
localStorage stores token + role
    ↓
navigate("/admin/home")
OR
navigate("/user/home")

2) SignUp Flow
SignUp.jsx
    ↓
authService.js
    ↓
Backend /auth/signup
    ↓
User stored in DB
    ↓
navigate("/signin")

3) Admin Add Book 
AdminHome.jsx
    ↓
react-hook-form validation
    ↓
adminService.js
    ↓
POST /books
    ↓
MySQL
    ↓
toast.success()
    ↓
fetchBooks()
    ↓
table refresh

4) Admin Book Delete Flow
Delete Button
    ↓
Modal Opens
    ↓
Confirm Yes
    ↓
DELETE /books/:id
    ↓
MySQL Delete
    ↓
Toast Success
    ↓
Reload Table

5) Admin Book Update Flow
Edit Button
    ↓
navigate("/admin/update-book/1")
    ↓
UpdateBook.jsx
    ↓
GET /books/:id
    ↓
Fill Form
    ↓
Update Button
    ↓
PUT /books/:id
    ↓
Redirect Admin Home

6) User Book Search Flow 
UserHome.jsx
    ↓
Search Form Submit
    ↓
GET /books/search
    ↓
Filtered Books
    ↓
Book Cards Updated

7) User Book add to Cart flow
Add To Cart Button
    ↓
POST /cart
    ↓
MySQL Cart Insert
    ↓
Toast Success

8) Payment Flow
Proceed To Payment
    ↓
POST /orders
    ↓
Order Created
    ↓
POST /payments
    ↓
Payment Success
    ↓
Toast

9) Cancel order Flow
Cancel Button
    ↓
DELETE /orders/:id
    ↓
DB Delete
    ↓
Reload Orders

10) Profile Flow
Profile.jsx

GET /users/profile

    ↓

11) Contact flow
